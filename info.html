Nightmare Maze: Survive the Horror

Welcome to the most terrifying maze experience ever created. In Nightmare Maze, you are trapped in an ever-changing labyrinth filled with unspeakable horrors. Your only goal: survive and escape.

Game Mechanics

Navigate through a procedurally generated maze where:

  • Each maze is unique and randomized
  • Survival time determines your ranking
  • Monsters hunt you with increasing intensity
  • Limited resources and tools are scattered throughout
  • Real-time tracking of your distance from exit and threats

Challenges

Survive increasingly difficult levels where:

  • Maze complexity increases
  • Monster AI becomes more aggressive
  • Your vision becomes more restricted
  • Sound and environmental traps activate
